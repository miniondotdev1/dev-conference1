Who should attend DevConf 2026?

DevConf is designed for students, software engineers, UI/UX designers, DevOps professionals, and tech leaders looking to learn, network, and grow their careers.

Will I receive a participation certificate?

Yes. Every registered attendee will receive a digital certificate after the conference concludes.

Are meals and refreshments included?

Yes. Complimentary lunch, coffee breaks, and refreshments are included with every conference pass.

Can I meet recruiters and hiring companies?

Absolutely! Visit the Career Hub to connect with recruiters, explore job opportunities, and participate in networking sessions throughout the event.

Can I transfer or cancel my ticket?

Ticket transfers are allowed before the registration deadline. Refund and cancellation policies are available on the registration page.
